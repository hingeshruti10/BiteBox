Welcome to Bite Box-Food Delivery App

**BiteBox** is a fast and reliable food delivery app designed to bring delicious meals right to your doorstep. The app connects users with their favorite restaurants, offering a wide variety of cuisines, easy online ordering, secure payment options, and real-time order tracking. BiteBox focuses on providing quick delivery, fresh food, and a smooth user experience. With its simple interface and smart features, BiteBox makes food ordering convenient, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone.
